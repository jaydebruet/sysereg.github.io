Syntax and Semantic Research Group is group of some young researchers of Bangladesh. Interests of this group is:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) : Goal of our research group is to process natural language to help machines. Word Problem Solving and Question Answering are the primary focus of this group.
  • Sentiment Analysis: We will develop softwares that will use sentiment analysis tool to predict based on the users requirements.
For more information about our research, please check SySeReG's projects and publications pages.
